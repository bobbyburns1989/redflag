PINK FLAG PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: November 10, 2025

Welcome to Pink Flag. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our app.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect minimal information to provide our service:

• Email Address: Used for account creation and authentication via Supabase
• Search Credits Balance: Track your remaining searches for credit-based access
• Purchase History: Transaction records for credit purchases via RevenueCat and Apple
• Usage Data: Basic app usage statistics to improve our service

IMPORTANT: We DO NOT store your search queries or search results. All searches are ephemeral and private.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your information to:

• Provide and improve the Pink Flag service
• Process your credit purchases
• Track your search credit balance
• Send important service updates
• Comply with legal obligations

We DO NOT:
• Store your search queries or results
• Build profiles based on your searches
• Aggregate or analyze your search data
• Share your search activity with anyone

3. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

• Your data is securely stored using Supabase (PostgreSQL database)
• All connections use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
• We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data
• We do not sell your personal information to third parties

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We use the following third-party services:

• Supabase: Database and authentication services
• RevenueCat: Payment processing and subscription management
• Apple App Store: Payment processing
• Offenders.io: Public registry search data (no personal data shared)

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their use of your information.

5. DATA RETENTION

• Account data: Retained until you delete your account
• Search queries: NOT retained - searches are ephemeral and private
• Search results: NOT retained - results are displayed only and not stored
• Purchase records: Retained for legal and accounting requirements (Apple/RevenueCat requirements)

6. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

• Access your personal data
• Correct inaccurate data
• Delete your account and data
• Export your data
• Opt-out of non-essential data collection

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Pink Flag is not intended for users under 17 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. DATA DELETION

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the Settings screen. This action:

• Permanently deletes your account
• Removes your email and authentication data
• Removes all transaction records (where legally permitted)
• Cannot be undone

Note: Search history is NOT stored, so there is nothing to delete. Your searches are private and ephemeral.

10. CONTACT US

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data:

Email: support@pinkflagapp.com
App: Settings > Legal & Support

11. GDPR COMPLIANCE (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:

• Right to access your data
• Right to rectification
• Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
• Right to restrict processing
• Right to data portability
• Right to object to processing

12. CCPA COMPLIANCE (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under CCPA:

• Right to know what data we collect
• Right to delete your data
• Right to opt-out of sale (we don't sell data)
• Right to non-discrimination

13. DISCLAIMER

Pink Flag provides access to PUBLIC RECORDS ONLY. The data we display:

• May be incomplete, outdated, or contain errors
• Should not be the sole basis for any decision
• Must be independently verified
• Is provided for personal safety awareness only

We are not responsible for:

• Accuracy of public registry data
• Actions taken based on search results
• Misuse of the application

14. INTERNATIONAL USERS

Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

By using Pink Flag, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this privacy policy and agree to its terms.
